Our History

The Theeke Timeline

From the very beginning to right now. Every family has a story — here’s ours in chronological order.

The 1960s

🧑🏻 Origins

The Theeke Name Begins in Green Bay

The family plants roots in Green Bay, Wisconsin. A small house on the north side, cold winters, and a neighborhood full of people you knew by first name.

The 1970s

👤 Family

Kids Fill the House

The family grows. The kitchen is never quiet. The garage becomes an art studio, a workshop, and eventually a place to hide from whoever's doing dishes.

🏈 Everyday

Little League Summers

Every summer Saturday belonged to Little League. The bleachers smelled like sunscreen and hot dogs, and there was always someone's grandpa keeping score in a notebook.

The 1980s

🎓 Milestone

First Graduation

The first Theeke kid crosses a graduation stage. The car ride home featured arguing about where to eat and someone crying while insisting they weren’t crying.

🏠 Milestone

A New Home

Moving day. Four trips with a borrowed pickup truck. Somebody dropped the couch on the porch steps. It stayed there for three days.

The 1990s

🍉 Holiday

The Annual Thanksgiving Wars Begin

Every year: whose turn is it to host, who’s bringing rolls, and why does somebody always show up with a dish nobody asked for. It became tradition.

🎨 Milestone

1995 — Artrageous Ink Opens

David opens Artrageous Ink at 1035 Main Street in downtown Green Bay. A family dream made real. What started as a small shop became Northeast Wisconsin’s premier tattoo studio — and a point of pride for every Theeke.

🎄 Holiday

1998 — The Christmas Blizzard

Eighteen inches overnight. Nobody goes anywhere. Card games, blanket forts, and the best Christmas in family history. Proof that the best plans are the ones that fail.

The 2000s

🏾 Funny

2001 — The Lawn Chair Incident (Fourth of July)

No further comment is available at this time. Uncle Gary has been informed of the permanent fireworks ban. He has not accepted it gracefully.

🚢 Vacation

2004 — Door County Camping Disaster

Tent, cooler, three sleeping bags. The tent lasted one night. Everyone slept in the van. The deer through the windshield at dawn is the stuff of legend. The s’mores were excellent.

⚽ Kids

2007 — The Soccer Season

Zero wins, massive trophies, infinite juice boxes. Everyone still has their trophy. Nobody knows what happened to the scorebook. It was probably intentional.

🍉 Holiday

2009 — The Pizza Thanksgiving

Frozen turkey. No time to thaw. Three family members claimed to have a plan. Zero plans were enacted. Domino’s was called. We ate at 9pm. Rated the best Thanksgiving of the decade.

The 2010s

🎉 Milestone

2012 — The Graduation Party

Grandpa’s speech. Questionable potato salad. Dancing on the deck. The neighbors called. We turned it down. We turned it back up. Still one of the best nights.

🦏 Funny

2014 — The Easter Dog Victory

46 out of 48 Easter eggs found by the dog before the kids went outside. The remaining 2 surfaced during June lawn mowing. The dog remains unapologetic.

🎶 Everyday

2016 — Sunday Morning, August

A Sunday that had no agenda. Records, pancakes, nobody leaving when they should have. One of those days that looks small in the calendar and massive in memory.

🏦 Milestone

2019 — Grandma’s 80th Birthday

“I don’t want a party.” We threw a party. She cried at the door, complained about the napkins, then danced for two hours straight. She later admitted it was “not terrible.”

The 2020s

📷 Milestone

2026 — This Website

We built a place to hold it all. The photos, the stories, the milestones, the disasters. A home for every memory we have and every one we haven’t made yet. Welcome.
