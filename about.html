About Artrageous Ink

History

Northeast Wisconsin's Premier Tattoo Studio Since 1995

Since opening in 1995, Artrageous Ink Tattoo Studio has become one of Northeast Wisconsin's best known and most respected tattoo shops. Founded by David Theeke, Rob Jensen and Eric Ebbole, the studio has grown into a team of experienced, versatile artists committed to creating exceptional work in a welcoming environment.

Located just blocks from downtown Green Bay, we specialize in all styles — black and grey, fine line, traditional, neo-traditional, Japanese, old school, new school, portraits, graffiti, and fine art tattoos. Our artists work from drawings, designs, or photographs you bring in, and they're dedicated to making your vision a reality.

We maintain a clean, safe, and sterile environment and emphasize customer service from the moment you walk in to the moment your tattoo is complete. Every client deserves to feel comfortable and informed throughout the process.

Artrageous Ink has been voted a "Best Tattoo Parlor" finalist by Green Bay Press Gazette readers for 19 consecutive years — a distinction we're proud of and motivated by every single day.
