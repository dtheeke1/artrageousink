Brady Buckman

About the Artist

Brady Buckman developed a lifelong passion for drawing and martial arts as a child. He completed his tattoo apprenticeship while attending art school in the late 1980s, studying fine art drawing simultaneously — a combination that shaped his precise, detail-oriented style.

After opening his own shop briefly, Brady traveled nationally and internationally before settling at Tattoos by Lou in Miami for 18 years, where mentor Ken Cameron made him manager. He eventually relocated to Green Bay to be near family and joined the Artrageous Ink team. Brady currently teaches Gracie Jiu Jitsu full-time while tattooing by appointment.

Brady tattoos by appointment only. Contact him directly or call the shop to schedule.
