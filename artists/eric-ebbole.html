Eric Ebbole

About the Artist

Eric Ebbole was taught tattooing by his father, Electric Ed Ebbole, and began his career in fall 1986 at Gulf Coast Tattoos in Mobile, Alabama. He has since worked internationally — across Atlanta, Florida, California, Amsterdam, and Germany — earning a reputation as one of the most well-traveled and experienced artists in the region.

Early in his career he was influenced by industry legends Paul Rogers, Jack Armstrong, and Don Nolan. He collaborates with renowned artists like Mike Dorsey and Shaun Anderson. Eric specializes in black and grey as well as color pieces across all sizes and genres. When he's not tattooing he enjoys traveling and spending time with the people he loves.
