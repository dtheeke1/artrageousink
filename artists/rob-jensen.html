Rob Jensen

About the Artist

Rob received his first tattoo at age 13 and never looked back. He apprenticed at Gold Coast Tattoos under Ed and Eric Ebbole for approximately 18 months, then worked at Tattoos by Lou in South Beach Miami for several years, honing his craft in one of the most competitive tattoo markets in the country.

In 1994, he and David Theeke opened Artrageous Ink in Green Bay — the city's second tattoo shop at the time. Rob has since traveled, worked conventions nationwide, and returned to Green Bay to be part of the team he helped build. He embraces all styles and values the creative challenge every new piece brings.
