Who We Are

Green Bay's Home for Custom Tattoo Art

Located blocks from downtown Green Bay, Artrageous Ink has been the premiere tattoo studio serving Northeast Wisconsin since 1995. Founded by David Theeke, Rob Jensen and Eric Ebbole, our studio has grown into one of the region's most respected shops.

We specialize in all styles — black and grey, fine line, traditional, neo-traditional, Japanese, old school, portraits, and custom fine art. Our artists work from your drawings, photos, or ideas you describe, and they're committed to getting it right.

Every client deserves a clean, safe, and comfortable experience. From the moment you walk in to the moment you leave, we're focused on you.
